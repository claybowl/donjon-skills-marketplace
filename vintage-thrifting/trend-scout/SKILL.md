# Trend Scout

Spot the next big thing before everyone else. Trend analysis for vintage resellers who want to stay ahead of the curve.

---

## What It Does

Predicts what's about to pop:
- **Trending Now** - What's hot this week
- **Rising Trends** - Early signals of next wave
- **Seasonal Forecasts** - What to stock for upcoming seasons
- **Platform-Specific** - What's selling where
- **Influencer Tracking** - What styles are gaining traction

---

## Inputs (from User Prompt)

| Input | Required | Description |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| `focus_area` | ❌ Optional | Specific category (denim, dresses, etc.) |
| `platform` | ❌ Optional | Poshmark, Depop, eBay, TikTok |
| `timeframe` | ❌ Optional | This week, next month, next season |

**Forecasted Trends:**

**Sourcing Strategy:**
- Buy at current prices

### 📉 DECLINING (Sell Now)

**Trends Past Peak:**
- Basic Y2K (low-rise without unique details)
- Generic cottagecore (over-saturated)
- Fast fashion "vintage-inspired"
- Generic 90s streetwear (without brand/story)

**Action:**
- Discount to move inventory
- Don't over-source
- Focus on unique pieces only

## Platform-Specific Trends

### Depop (Gen Z Focus)

**Dominating:**
- Y2K and 90s revival
- Grunge and alternative
- Cottagecore and fairycore
- Streetwear (Nike, Adidas vintage)
- Unique statement pieces

**Emerging:**
- Boho revival
- Vintage athletic
- DIY/customized items
- 70s disco influence

**Strategy:**
- Model photos essential
- Aesthetic staging
- Trending hashtags: #coquette #blokecore #gorpcore

---

### Poshmark (Millennial Focus)

**Dominating:**
- Designer vintage (accessible luxury)
- Workwear and professional vintage
- Classic brands (J.Crew, Banana Republic vintage)
- Athletic wear (Lululemon, Athleta)
- Plus-size vintage (underserved market)

**Emerging:**
- Quiet luxury aesthetic
- Vintage investment pieces
- Sustainable fashion stories

**Strategy:**
- Bundle-friendly items
- Detailed measurements
- "Closet clear out" participation

---

### eBay (Collector/Broad Market)

**Dominating:**
- True vintage (pre-1980s)
- Band merchandise
- Designer vintage (authenticated)
- Vintage workwear
- Niche collectibles

**Emerging:**
- 90s nostalgia items
- Vintage tech accessories
- Deadstock items

**Strategy:**
- Accurate dating
- Historical context
- International shipping

## Trend Analysis Workflow

### Step 1: Monitor Multiple Sources

Check weekly:
- Social media hashtags
- Fashion week coverage
- Celebrity/influencer posts
- Platform best-sellers

### Step 2: Identify Early Signals

Look for:
- Emerging hashtags
- New aesthetic terms
- Celebrity adoption
- Price increases on specific items

### Step 3: Validate with Data

Confirm:
- Search volume increasing
- Sales velocity accelerating
- Prices trending up
- Inventory scarcity

### Step 4: Adjust Sourcing

Action:
- Prioritize rising items
- Stock up before peak
- Clear declining inventory
- Price ahead of trend

## Common Gotchas & Solutions

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Regional variation | Check local trends, not just national |
| Platform differences | Trends hit Depop before Poshmark |
| False signals | Validate with sales data, not just buzz |
| Timing | Buy early, but not too early |
