# last30days-skill

“Agent-led search engine” ranked by upvotes/likes/real money (incl. Polymarket). Researches across Reddit, X, YouTube, HN, web; v3 resolves “who matters” first (finds the right creator/subreddit/channels) before searching.

## Triggers
- "last 30 days"
- "recent trends"
- "viral content last month"
- "social media recap"
- "trending last 30 days"
- "agent led search"

## Description
last30days-skill is an agent-led search engine that researches trending topics and content from the last 30 days across multiple platforms including Reddit, X (Twitter), YouTube, Hacker News, and the wider web. Version 3 improves relevance by first identifying key creators, subreddits, and channels before conducting searches, ensuring results come from authoritative sources.

## Features
- Multi-platform research: Reddit, X, YouTube, HN, web
- Temporal focus: last 30 days
- Authority-first approach: identifies key creators/subreddits/channels before searching
- Ranking by engagement: upvotes, likes, and real money (Polymarket)
- Trend identification and tracking
- Cross-platform correlation
- Time-bound analysis

## Usage
Use when you need to research what's been trending or important over the last month across social media and online communities.

## Example
```
/last30days-skill show trending topics in ai
/last30days-skill research viral content last month
/last30days-skill find influential creators in cryptocurrency
/last30days-skill analyze hacker news trends past 30 days
```
